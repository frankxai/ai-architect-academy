## ‚ö†Ô∏è Risks & Mitigation

### Technical Risks

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| **Model performance below expectations** | Medium | High | Extensive testing, fallback models, continuous improvement |
| **Data quality issues** | High | High | Data quality initiative, governance, validation pipelines |
| **Integration complexity** | Medium | Medium | API-first architecture, staged rollout, dedicated integration team |
| **Scalability challenges** | Low | High | Cloud-native design, load testing, auto-scaling |
| **Security vulnerabilities** | Low | Critical | Security-first design, penetration testing, compliance audits |

### Business Risks

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| **Executive sponsorship fades** | Medium | Critical | Regular business value updates, executive steering committee |
| **Talent acquisition fails** | High | High | Competitive comp, upskilling programs, strategic partnerships |
| **Budget cuts** | Low | High | Quick wins for credibility, phased investment, ROI tracking |
| **Regulatory changes** | Medium | High | Legal review, compliance-first design, flexibility built-in |
| **Competitor leapfrog** | Medium | Medium | Continuous innovation, market monitoring, agile pivots |

### Ethical & Governance Risks

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| **Bias in AI models** | Medium | High | Bias detection, diverse training data, ethics review board |
| **Privacy violations** | Low | Critical | Privacy-by-design, data minimization, compliance automation |
| **Explainability gaps** | Medium | Medium | Interpretable models, audit trails, human oversight |
| **EU AI Act non-compliance** | Low | Critical | Compliance framework, legal review, third-party audits |
