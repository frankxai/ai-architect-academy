## Agenda

### Pre-Workshop (1 week before)
- [ ] Send calendar invite with objectives
- [ ] Share pre-read materials
- [ ] Request attendees prepare examples
- [ ] Set up virtual meeting room (if remote)
- [ ] Prepare slide deck and materials

### Part 1: Introduction & Context (30 min)

**9:00 - 9:10 | Welcome & Introductions** (10 min)
- Round-table introductions
- Roles and responsibilities
- Workshop objectives overview
- Agenda review

**9:10 - 9:30 | Business Context** (20 min)
- Current business situation
- Strategic priorities
- Key challenges and pain points
- Success metrics that matter

**Discussion Questions:**
- What are your top 3 business priorities this year?
- What's working well today?
- What's not working or needs improvement?
- How do you measure success?

### Part 2: Discovery & Exploration (60 min)

**9:30 - 10:00 | Pain Points Deep Dive** (30 min)
- Specific operational challenges
- Manual processes and bottlenecks
- Data and insight gaps
- Customer experience issues

**Discovery Questions:**
- Walk me through your current process for [key workflow]
- Where do you spend the most time?
- What decisions require more data or insights?
- What frustrates your team the most?
- What frustrates your customers?

**10:00 - 10:30 | AI Opportunities Brainstorm** (30 min)
- Review potential AI use cases
- Match capabilities to pain points
- Prioritization discussion
- Quick feasibility assessment

**Brainstorm Prompts:**
- If you could automate one thing, what would it be?
- Where do you need better predictions or insights?
- What customer interactions could be improved?
- What data do you have but not using?

**10:30 - 10:40 | Break** (10 min)

### Part 3: Architecture Co-Design (60 min)

**10:40 - 11:10 | Use Case Refinement** (30 min)
- Select top 3 use cases
- Define inputs, outputs, users
- Sketch high-level architecture
- Identify data requirements

**For each use case:**
- **Who** will use it?
- **What** problem does it solve?
- **When** will they use it?
- **Where** does it fit in workflow?
- **How** will success be measured?

**11:10 - 11:40 | Technical Discussion** (30 min)
- Technology stack options
- Integration points
- Data availability and quality
- Infrastructure requirements
- Security and compliance needs

**Architecture Questions:**
- What systems need to integrate?
- What data is available and where?
- What are your security requirements?
- Cloud or on-premise preference?
- Any compliance constraints (GDPR, HIPAA, etc.)?

### Part 4: Planning & Next Steps (30 min)

**11:40 - 12:00 | Risks & Dependencies** (20 min)
- Identify potential blockers
- Technical risks
- Organizational risks
- Resource constraints
- Timeline considerations

**Risk Assessment:**
- What could prevent success?
- What do we need that we don't have?
- Who needs to approve this?
- What's the timeline pressure?

**12:00 - 12:10 | Prioritization** (10 min)
- Rank use cases by value and feasibility
- Agree on #1 priority
- Quick wins vs. strategic bets
- Sequencing dependencies

**Prioritization Criteria:**
- Business value (High/Medium/Low)
- Technical feasibility (High/Medium/Low)
- Time to value (Weeks/Months/Quarters)
- Resource requirements (Light/Medium/Heavy)

**12:10 - 12:20 | Next Steps & Actions** (10 min)
- Define immediate next steps
- Assign owners and dates
- Follow-up meetings
- Documentation deliverables

**Action Items:**
- [ ] [Action 1 - Owner - Date]
- [ ] [Action 2 - Owner - Date]
- [ ] [Action 3 - Owner - Date]

**12:20 - 12:30 | Closing & Q&A** (10 min)
- Recap key decisions
- Confirm next meeting
- Open questions
- Thank attendees
