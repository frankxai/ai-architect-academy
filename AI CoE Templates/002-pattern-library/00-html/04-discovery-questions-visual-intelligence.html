Discovery Questions: Visual Intelligence & Automation

  • Pattern Number: 04
  • Pattern Name: Visual Intelligence & Automation
  • Document Type: Discovery Questions
  • Created Date: 2025-08-14
  • Version: 1.0

Discovery & Current State Assessment

  1. What visual inspection, monitoring, or analysis tasks does your organization currently perform manually, and how much time and resources are dedicated to these activities?

  2. Purpose: Identify automation opportunities and establish baseline for 80% time reduction

  3. What types of visual content do you work with (manufacturing quality control, security surveillance, medical imaging, inventory tracking, agricultural monitoring), and what are your current accuracy rates?

  4. Purpose: Understand visual intelligence use cases and current performance benchmarks

  5. How do you currently capture, store, and analyze visual data (images, videos), and what systems are involved in your visual workflows?

  6. Purpose: Assess current visual data infrastructure and integration requirements

Strategy & Business Alignment

  1. What are your organization's operational efficiency and quality improvement goals, and how important is automated visual analysis to achieving these objectives?

  2. Purpose: Align solution capabilities with operational strategy

  3. Which business areas (manufacturing, security, healthcare, retail, agriculture) would benefit most from intelligent visual automation, and what would be your priority use cases?

  4. Purpose: Prioritize implementation approach and identify highest-impact applications

  5. How do you currently measure quality, accuracy, and efficiency in visual tasks, and what KPIs would you use to evaluate AI-powered visual intelligence?

  6. Purpose: Establish success criteria and performance benchmarks

Opportunity Qualification

  1. What is your annual budget for quality control, security systems, inspection equipment, and automation technology, and what ROI do you expect from visual intelligence investments?

  2. Purpose: Qualify budget authority and establish value expectations

  3. Who are the key stakeholders (operations managers, quality directors, security leaders, IT executives) who would need to approve visual intelligence solutions?

  4. Purpose: Identify buying committee and operational champions

  5. What is your preferred timeline for implementing visual intelligence capabilities, and are there any compliance, safety, or operational deadlines driving this timeline?

  6. Purpose: Understand urgency and regulatory requirements

Technical Scoping & Architecture

  1. What are your visual data volumes (images/videos per day, resolution requirements, real-time vs. batch processing needs) and what processing speed expectations do you have?

    • Purpose: Size GPU compute resources (A100/H100) and processing requirements

  2. What existing systems (manufacturing equipment, security cameras, medical devices, inventory systems) would need to integrate with the visual intelligence platform?

    • Purpose: Plan Oracle Integration and system connectivity requirements

  3. Do you have specific requirements for model training, custom visual recognition, and accuracy thresholds for different types of visual analysis?

    • Purpose: Configure OCI Data Science platform and computer vision model development

Data & Storage

  1. What are your visual data storage requirements, retention policies, and access patterns, and do you need to maintain historical visual data for compliance or analysis?

    • Purpose: Design OCI Object Storage architecture and data lifecycle management

  2. What are your data security and privacy requirements for visual content, particularly for sensitive applications like medical imaging or security surveillance?

    • Purpose: Configure appropriate security controls and access management

Performance & Accuracy

  1. What accuracy levels do you require for different visual analysis tasks, and how do you currently handle exceptions, false positives, and quality assurance in visual processes?
    • Purpose: Design validation workflows and exception handling processes
