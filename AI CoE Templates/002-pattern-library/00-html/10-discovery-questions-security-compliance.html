Discovery Questions: AI-Powered Security & Compliance

Discovery & Current State Assessment

  1. What security threats and compliance challenges does your organization currently face, and how do you currently detect, respond to, and report on security incidents?

  2. Purpose: Identify security gaps and understand current threat detection capabilities

  3. What is your current security incident response time and threat detection accuracy, and how much do you spend annually on security operations and compliance activities?

  4. Purpose: Establish baseline for 99% detection rate, 85% response time reduction, and 50% compliance cost savings

  5. What security tools, SIEM systems, and compliance frameworks are you currently using, and what are the main limitations of your existing security infrastructure?

  6. Purpose: Assess current security stack and integration requirements

Strategy & Business Alignment

  1. What are your organization's security posture and risk management goals, and how important is proactive threat detection to your business continuity strategy?

  2. Purpose: Align solution capabilities with security strategy and risk tolerance

  3. Which business areas (financial services, healthcare, government, retail, energy, technology) have the highest security and compliance requirements, and what would be your priority use cases?

  4. Purpose: Prioritize implementation approach and identify critical security applications

  5. How do you currently measure security effectiveness, compliance adherence, and incident impact, and what KPIs would you use to evaluate AI-powered security solutions?

  6. Purpose: Establish success criteria and security performance benchmarks

Opportunity Qualification

  1. What is your annual budget for cybersecurity tools, compliance activities, and security personnel, and what cost savings do you expect from automated security operations?

  2. Purpose: Qualify budget authority and establish ROI expectations

  3. Who are the key stakeholders (CISO, security directors, compliance officers, risk managers) who would need to approve AI-powered security solutions?

  4. Purpose: Identify buying committee and security champions

  5. What is your preferred timeline for implementing enhanced security capabilities, and are there any regulatory deadlines or security incidents driving this timeline?

  6. Purpose: Understand urgency and compliance requirements

Technical Scoping & Architecture

  1. What are your security data volumes (logs per day, events per second, data retention requirements) and what real-time processing capabilities do you need for threat detection?

    • Purpose: Size OCI Logging, Cloud Guard, and processing infrastructure

  2. What existing security systems (SIEM, firewalls, databases, applications) would need to integrate with the AI-powered security platform?

    • Purpose: Plan OCI Events integration and security ecosystem connectivity

  3. Do you have requirements for custom threat detection models, industry-specific security patterns, and automated response workflows?

    • Purpose: Configure OCI Anomaly Detection and automated remediation capabilities

Compliance & Regulatory

  1. What regulatory compliance requirements do you need to meet (SOX, HIPAA, PCI DSS, GDPR, NERC, industry-specific), and what audit and reporting capabilities do you require?

    • Purpose: Design compliance monitoring and automated reporting features

  2. What are your data classification, encryption, and access control requirements, particularly for sensitive data and critical systems?

    • Purpose: Configure OCI Data Safe, Vault, and access management controls

Incident Response & Operations

  1. What automated response capabilities do you want (threat isolation, user blocking, system patching, alert escalation), and what level of human oversight do you require for security actions?
    • Purpose: Design OCI Functions automation and security operations workflows

This comprehensive discovery process enables organizations to build a robust AI-powered security and compliance framework that proactively protects against threats while ensuring regulatory adherence and operational efficiency.
