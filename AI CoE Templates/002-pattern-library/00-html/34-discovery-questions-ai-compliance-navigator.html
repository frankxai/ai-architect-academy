Discovery & Current State Assessment

  1. What regulatory frameworks and standards must your organization comply with?

  2. Purpose: Inventory all applicable regulations and prioritize implementation

  3. How do you currently manage and track regulatory compliance?

  4. Purpose: Assess current maturity and identify improvement opportunities

  5. How much time and resources do you spend on compliance activities annually?

  6. Purpose: Quantify current costs and potential savings

Strategy & Business Alignment

  1. What are your biggest compliance challenges (cost, complexity, changes, audits)?

  2. Purpose: Identify primary pain points to address

  3. How does compliance impact your business operations and growth?

  4. Purpose: Understand business impact and strategic importance

  5. What would be the impact of a major compliance violation?

  6. Purpose: Quantify risk exposure and business case

Opportunity Qualification

  1. Have you experienced any compliance violations or audit findings recently?

  2. Purpose: Understand compliance history and urgency

  3. Who owns compliance in your organization, and what is their budget?

  4. Purpose: Identify stakeholders and financial commitment

  5. What is driving the need for compliance automation (growth, regulation, cost)?

  6. Purpose: Understand business drivers and timeline

Technical Scoping & Architecture

  1. What GRC or compliance tools do you currently use?

    • Purpose: Assess existing tools and integration requirements

  2. How are your compliance policies and controls documented today?

    • Purpose: Evaluate documentation maturity and migration needs

  3. What systems need to be monitored for compliance?

    • Purpose: Scope integration and monitoring requirements

Regulatory Coverage

  1. Which jurisdictions do you operate in, and what are their requirements?

    • Purpose: Determine geographic compliance scope

  2. How do you stay updated on regulatory changes?

    • Purpose: Assess regulatory intelligence capabilities

  3. Do you have industry-specific regulations (healthcare, finance, etc.)?

    • Purpose: Identify specialized compliance needs

Control Management

  1. How many controls do you currently manage, and how are they organized?

    • Purpose: Understand control complexity and structure

  2. How do you map controls to multiple regulatory frameworks?

    • Purpose: Assess framework harmonization needs

  3. What is your process for implementing new controls?

    • Purpose: Design control deployment workflows

Evidence & Audit

  1. How do you collect and manage evidence of compliance?

    • Purpose: Design evidence collection automation

  2. How many audits do you undergo annually, and what is the preparation effort?

    • Purpose: Quantify audit burden and automation value

  3. What are your evidence retention requirements?

    • Purpose: Design retention and archival policies

Risk Management

  1. How do you assess and prioritize compliance risks?

    • Purpose: Design risk scoring and prioritization

  2. What is your risk tolerance for compliance violations?

    • Purpose: Configure alerting and escalation thresholds

  3. How do you manage third-party compliance risks?

    • Purpose: Scope vendor compliance management

Monitoring & Testing

  1. How frequently do you test control effectiveness?

    • Purpose: Design testing automation and frequency

  2. What is your process for detecting compliance drift?

    • Purpose: Plan continuous monitoring approach

  3. How do you handle compliance exceptions and waivers?

    • Purpose: Design exception management workflows

Reporting & Analytics

  1. What compliance reports do you generate, and for whom?

    • Purpose: Design reporting templates and distribution

  2. What compliance metrics are most important to leadership?

    • Purpose: Configure executive dashboards and KPIs

  3. How do you demonstrate compliance to regulators?

    • Purpose: Design regulatory reporting capabilities

Data Management

  1. Where does compliance-relevant data reside in your organization?

    • Purpose: Plan data integration and collection

  2. What are your data privacy and sovereignty requirements?

  3. How do you manage sensitive data for compliance?

Integration Requirements

Cloud & Infrastructure

  3. What are your high availability requirements for compliance systems?

Change Management

  1. How do you manage regulatory change implementation?

    • Purpose: Design change management workflows

Future Considerations
