Discovery Questions: Language Understanding & Communication

  • Pattern Number: 02
  • Pattern Name: Language Understanding & Communication
  • Document Type: Discovery Questions
  • Created Date: 2025-08-14
  • Version: 1.0

Discovery & Current State Assessment

  1. What communication challenges does your organization face across different languages, regions, or customer segments, and how are you currently handling multilingual support?

  2. Purpose: Identify language barriers and understand current translation/communication processes

  3. What is your current customer service setup, and what percentage of inquiries could potentially be handled by intelligent chatbots or automated systems?

  4. Purpose: Establish baseline for the 60% efficiency improvement and automation opportunities

  5. How do you currently process and analyze customer communications (emails, chats, calls), and what insights are you extracting from these interactions?

  6. Purpose: Assess current language processing capabilities and integration needs

Strategy & Business Alignment

  1. What are your organization's customer experience and global expansion goals, and how important is seamless multilingual communication to your strategy?

  2. Purpose: Align solution capabilities with customer experience and internationalization objectives

  3. Which business functions (customer service, healthcare documentation, compliance, global collaboration) would benefit most from intelligent language understanding?

  4. Purpose: Prioritize implementation approach and identify high-impact use cases

  5. How do you currently measure customer satisfaction and communication effectiveness, and what KPIs would you use to evaluate AI-powered language solutions?

  6. Purpose: Establish success criteria and customer experience benchmarks

Opportunity Qualification

  1. What is your annual budget for customer service technology, translation services, and communication tools, and what cost savings do you expect from automation?

  2. Purpose: Qualify budget authority and establish ROI expectations

  3. Who are the key stakeholders (customer service leaders, global operations, compliance officers) who would need to approve and champion language understanding solutions?

  4. Purpose: Identify buying committee and operational champions

  5. What is your preferred timeline for implementing intelligent communication capabilities, and are there any customer service or compliance deadlines driving this timeline?

  6. Purpose: Understand urgency and regulatory requirements

Technical Scoping & Architecture

  1. What are your communication volume characteristics (daily interactions, languages supported, real-time vs. batch processing needs) and response time expectations?

    • Purpose: Size OCI Speech and streaming processing requirements

  2. What existing customer service platforms, CRM systems, and communication channels would need to integrate with the language understanding solution?

    • Purpose: Plan Oracle Digital Assistant integration and multi-system coordination

  3. Do you have specific requirements for conversation history, analytics, and reporting on communication patterns and customer sentiment?

    • Purpose: Configure Oracle Database 23ai for conversation intelligence and insights

Compliance & Security

  1. What are your data privacy, security, and regulatory compliance requirements for customer communications, particularly in regulated industries?

    • Purpose: Design appropriate security architecture and compliance monitoring

  2. Do you have specific requirements for conversation recording, audit trails, and data retention for customer interactions?

    • Purpose: Configure monitoring and compliance features

Training & Customization

  1. What industry-specific terminology, processes, and communication patterns would need to be incorporated into the language models, and what level of customization do you require?
    • Purpose: Plan OCI Data Science model training and domain-specific customization requirements
