Pattern Selection Guide

Overview

This guide helps you select the most appropriate AI design pattern for your specific business requirements and technical constraints.

Pattern Categories

1. Intelligent Operations

When to Use: Optimize business operations, automate decision-making, enhance operational efficiency - Customer Experience Patterns: Content generation, language understanding, personalization - Decision Support: AI-powered recommendations and analytics - Workflow Orchestration: Automated process management - Predictive Operations: Forecasting and proactive management - Autonomous Optimization: Self-optimizing systems

2. Platform Enablement

When to Use: Build foundational AI capabilities, enable rapid development, ensure compliance - Rapid Innovation: Accelerate AI development and deployment - Security & Compliance: Automated governance and risk management - Synthetic Data: Privacy-preserving data generation

3. AI Infrastructure

When to Use: Scale AI operations, manage ML lifecycles, optimize performance - Multicloud Orchestration: Distribute AI workloads across clouds - Model Lifecycle Management: End-to-end ML operations - AI Governance: Compliance and ethical AI management - Performance Optimization: High-performance AI computing

4. Industry-Specific Intelligence

Regulatory Intelligence

When to Use: Navigate complex regulatory environments, ensure compliance automation - AI Compliance Navigator: Automated regulatory compliance - Privacy-Preserving Analytics: Secure multi-party computation

Genomic Public Health Intelligence

When to Use: Process genomic data, track disease patterns, support public health - Genomic Sequence Processing: High-throughput genomic analysis - Pathogen Evolution Tracking: Disease surveillance and outbreak response

Energy Distribution Intelligence

When to Use: Optimize energy systems, manage smart grids, improve distribution - Geographic Information Systems: Spatial analytics for energy networks - Energy Market Trading: Optimization and risk management

Cyber Defense & Resilience

When to Use: Enhance cybersecurity, automate threat response, build resilient systems - AI-Powered Vulnerability Management: Proactive security threat management

5. Business Process Intelligence

When to Use: Transform specific business processes with AI - Insurance Rate Modeling: Advanced actuarial analytics

Selection Criteria

1. Business Objectives

  • Operational Efficiency: Choose Intelligent Operations patterns
  • Customer Experience: Focus on Customer Experience patterns
  • Compliance & Risk: Select Regulatory Intelligence patterns
  • Industry-Specific: Choose domain-specific intelligence patterns

2. Technical Maturity

  • Beginner: Start with Platform Enablement patterns
  • Intermediate: Implement Intelligent Operations patterns
  • Advanced: Deploy AI Infrastructure and Industry-Specific patterns

3. Data Requirements

  • Structured Data: Business Process Intelligence patterns

  • Sensitive Data: Privacy-Preserving Analytics patterns

4. Regulatory Environment
