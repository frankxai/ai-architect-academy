Discovery Questions: Intelligent Document Processing

Discovery & Current State Assessment

  1. What types of documents does your organization process most frequently (insurance claims, loan applications, medical records, contracts, tax documents), and what is your current processing workflow?

  2. Purpose: Identify high-volume document types and understand existing processes

  3. How much time and resources do you currently spend on document processing and data entry, and what are your current accuracy rates and error types?

  4. Purpose: Establish baseline for 80% time reduction and 95% accuracy improvement claims

  5. What document formats do you work with (PDFs, scanned images, handwritten forms, structured forms), and how do you currently extract and validate data from these documents?

  6. Purpose: Assess document complexity and current extraction capabilities

Strategy & Business Alignment

  1. What are your organization's digital transformation and operational efficiency goals, and how important is document automation to your competitive advantage?

  2. Purpose: Align solution capabilities with business strategy and transformation objectives

  3. Which business functions (insurance, banking, healthcare, legal, government, real estate) would benefit most from intelligent document processing, and what would be your priority use cases?

  4. Purpose: Prioritize implementation approach and identify highest-impact applications

  5. How do you currently measure document processing efficiency, accuracy, and customer satisfaction, and what KPIs would you use to evaluate automated document processing?

  6. Purpose: Establish success criteria and performance benchmarks

Opportunity Qualification

  1. What is your annual budget for document processing operations, data entry services, and process automation technology, and what cost savings do you expect from automation?

  2. Purpose: Qualify budget authority and establish ROI expectations

  3. Who are the key stakeholders (operations managers, compliance officers, IT leaders, business process owners) who would need to approve document processing automation?

  4. Purpose: Identify buying committee and process champions

  5. What is your preferred timeline for implementing intelligent document processing, and are there any regulatory, compliance, or business deadlines driving this timeline?

  6. Purpose: Understand urgency and compliance requirements

Technical Scoping & Architecture

  1. What are your document processing volumes (documents per day/month, peak periods, batch vs. real-time processing needs) and what processing speed requirements do you have?

    • Purpose: Size OCI Document Understanding and processing infrastructure

  2. What existing business systems (CRM, ERP, case management, databases) would need to integrate with the document processing solution for data flow and workflow automation?

    • Purpose: Plan Oracle Integration Cloud connectivity and API Gateway architecture

  3. Do you have requirements for custom document templates, industry-specific extraction rules, and validation workflows for different document types?

    • Purpose: Configure document understanding models and processing logic

Compliance & Quality Assurance

  1. What are your regulatory compliance requirements (HIPAA, SOX, GDPR, industry-specific regulations) for document handling, data extraction, and audit trails?

    • Purpose: Design compliant processing workflows and audit capabilities

  2. What quality assurance processes do you need for document processing, including human review workflows, exception handling, and accuracy validation?

    • Purpose: Configure validation processes and exception management

Data Management & Integration

  1. How do you want to handle extracted document data (direct system integration, data lakes, reporting databases), and what are your document archival and retrieval requirements?
    • Purpose: Plan Oracle Database 23ai integration and OCI Object Storage architecture for document management
