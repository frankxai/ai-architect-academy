Discovery Questions: Energy Market Trading & Optimization

  • Pattern Number: 21
  • Pattern Name: Energy Market Trading & Optimization
  • Document Type: Discovery Questions
  • Created Date: 2025-08-14

Discovery & Current State Assessment

  1. What energy commodities and markets do you currently trade, and what is your average daily trading volume?

  2. Purpose: Understand trading scope, scale, and market coverage requirements

  3. What trading platforms, order management systems, and market data feeds do you use today?

  4. Purpose: Assess current technology stack and integration requirements

  5. How do you currently execute trades - manually, semi-automated, or fully algorithmic?

  6. Purpose: Determine automation maturity and transformation scope

Strategy & Business Alignment

  1. What are your primary trading strategies (arbitrage, hedging, speculation, market making)?

  2. Purpose: Align AI/ML capabilities with specific trading strategy requirements

  3. What percentage improvement in trading profitability would justify this investment?

  4. Purpose: Establish ROI expectations and success metrics

  5. How do you measure trading performance today, and what KPIs are most important?

  6. Purpose: Define success criteria and performance benchmarks

Opportunity Qualification

  1. What is driving the need for advanced trading capabilities (competition, regulation, profitability)?

  2. Purpose: Understand business drivers and urgency

  3. Who are the key stakeholders in trading decisions, risk management, and technology investments?

  4. Purpose: Identify decision makers and approval process

  5. What budget range has been allocated for trading platform modernization?

  6. Purpose: Qualify financial commitment and scope appropriately

Technical Scoping & Architecture

  1. What is your current trading infrastructure (on-premise, cloud, hybrid)?

    • Purpose: Assess infrastructure modernization requirements

  2. What are your latency requirements for market data processing and order execution?

    • Purpose: Determine performance requirements and architecture needs

  3. How many traders, analysts, and risk managers will use the platform?

    • Purpose: Size user access and interface requirements

Market Data & Analytics

  1. Which market data providers and exchanges do you need to connect with?

    • Purpose: Plan data integration and connectivity requirements

  2. What types of analytics and predictions would provide the most value (price, demand, weather)?

    • Purpose: Prioritize ML model development efforts

  3. Do you currently use any quantitative models or algorithmic trading strategies?

    • Purpose: Assess analytical maturity and migration requirements

Risk Management

  1. What risk management framework and limits do you currently have in place?

    • Purpose: Design appropriate risk controls and monitoring

  2. How do you currently calculate and monitor Value at Risk (VaR) and other risk metrics?

    • Purpose: Understand risk calculation requirements and frequency

  3. Have you experienced any significant trading losses or risk events in the past?

    • Purpose: Identify specific risk areas to address

Regulatory & Compliance

  1. Which regulatory requirements must you comply with (REMIT, EMIR, MiFID II, FERC)?

    • Purpose: Design compliance controls and reporting capabilities

  2. What are your current regulatory reporting obligations and deadlines?

    • Purpose: Plan automated reporting workflows

  3. Do you require trade surveillance for market manipulation detection?

    • Purpose: Implement appropriate surveillance capabilities

Portfolio & Position Management

  1. How do you currently manage portfolio optimization and rebalancing?

    • Purpose: Design portfolio management capabilities

  2. What types of energy assets do you hold (physical, financial, derivatives)?

    • Purpose: Understand portfolio complexity and valuation requirements

  3. How do you manage cross-commodity and cross-market positions?

    • Purpose: Design integrated position management

Integration & Operations

  1. What other systems need integration (ERP, settlement, credit, treasury)?

    • Purpose: Plan enterprise integration architecture

  2. Do you trade physical energy requiring logistics and scheduling integration?

    • Purpose: Assess physical trading requirements

  3. What are your disaster recovery and business continuity requirements?

    • Purpose: Design high availability architecture

Renewable Energy & Sustainability

  1. What percentage of your trading involves renewable energy or green certificates?

    • Purpose: Assess renewable-specific requirements

  2. Do you participate in capacity markets, ancillary services, or flexibility trading?

    • Purpose: Understand new market participation needs

  3. How important are ESG considerations in your trading decisions?

    • Purpose: Incorporate sustainability metrics into trading logic

Performance & Scalability

  1. What are your peak trading volumes during volatile market conditions?

    • Purpose: Design for peak capacity requirements

  2. Do you require 24/7 trading capabilities across global markets?

    • Purpose: Plan for continuous operations and support

  3. What are your data retention requirements for historical trades and market data?

    • Purpose: Design appropriate data storage and archival

Advanced Capabilities

  1. Are you interested in high-frequency trading or market making strategies?

    • Purpose: Determine ultra-low latency requirements

  2. Would you benefit from natural language processing of news and market reports?

    • Purpose: Assess need for sentiment analysis capabilities

  3. Do you want to incorporate alternative data (satellite, IoT, social media)?

    • Purpose: Plan for alternative data integration

These discovery questions are designed to comprehensively assess an organization's readiness for implementing the Energy Market Trading & Optimization pattern.
