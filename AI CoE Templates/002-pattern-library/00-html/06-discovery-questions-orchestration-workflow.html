Discovery Questions: Intelligent Orchestration & Workflow Automation

  • Pattern Number: 06
  • Pattern Name: Intelligent Orchestration & Workflow Automation
  • Document Type: Discovery Questions
  • Created Date: 2025-08-14
  • Version: 1.0

Discovery & Current State Assessment

  1. What complex business processes does your organization currently manage manually (loan processing, patient care coordination, supply chain fulfillment, employee onboarding), and how many handoffs and approvals are involved?

  2. Purpose: Identify automation opportunities and understand current process complexity

  3. How long do your current end-to-end processes take to complete, and where do you see the biggest bottlenecks, delays, or manual interventions?

  4. Purpose: Establish baseline for 65% completion time reduction and 90% manual handoff elimination

  5. What systems and applications are involved in your business processes, and how do you currently coordinate activities across different departments and platforms?

  6. Purpose: Assess integration complexity and orchestration requirements

Strategy & Business Alignment

  1. What are your organization's operational efficiency and customer experience goals, and how important is process automation to your competitive differentiation?

  2. Purpose: Align solution capabilities with operational strategy and customer satisfaction objectives

  3. Which business areas (financial services, healthcare, supply chain, HR, government, insurance) would benefit most from intelligent workflow orchestration, and what would be your priority processes?

  4. Purpose: Prioritize implementation approach and identify highest-impact workflows

  5. How do you currently measure process efficiency, compliance adherence, and customer satisfaction, and what KPIs would you use to evaluate workflow automation success?

  6. Purpose: Establish success criteria and process performance benchmarks

Opportunity Qualification

  1. What is your annual budget for process improvement, business process management tools, and operational efficiency initiatives, and what cost savings do you expect from automation?

  2. Purpose: Qualify budget authority and establish ROI expectations

  3. Who are the key stakeholders (operations managers, process owners, compliance officers, IT leaders) who would need to approve workflow orchestration solutions?

  4. Purpose: Identify buying committee and process champions

  5. What is your preferred timeline for implementing intelligent workflow automation, and are there any regulatory, compliance, or customer experience pressures driving this timeline?

  6. Purpose: Understand urgency and business requirements

Technical Scoping & Architecture

  1. What are your process volumes (transactions per day, concurrent workflows, peak processing periods) and what performance requirements do you have for process completion times?

    • Purpose: Size Oracle Integration and processing infrastructure requirements

  2. What existing business systems (ERP, CRM, databases, legacy applications) would need to integrate with the workflow orchestration platform?

    • Purpose: Plan OCI Connector Hub integration and system connectivity

  3. Do you have requirements for real-time process monitoring, dynamic workflow adjustment, and intelligent decision-making within automated processes?

    • Purpose: Configure OCI Events, Streaming, and AI-driven process optimization

Compliance & Governance

  1. What compliance and audit requirements do you have for business processes (SOX, regulatory approvals, industry standards), and what audit trails and documentation do you need to maintain?

  2. Purpose: Design compliance monitoring and audit trail capabilities

  3. What approval workflows, escalation procedures, and exception handling processes do you need to maintain within automated workflows?

  4. Purpose: Configure human oversight and exception management processes

User Experience & Change Management

  1. How do you want users to interact with automated workflows (self-service portals, mobile apps, conversational interfaces), and what change management support will you need for process automation adoption?
    • Purpose: Plan Oracle Digital Assistant integration and user experience design

These discovery questions are designed to comprehensively assess an organization's readiness for implementing intelligent orchestration and workflow automation systems. The responses will help determine the appropriate implementation approach, identify potential challenges, and customize the solution to meet specific business needs.
