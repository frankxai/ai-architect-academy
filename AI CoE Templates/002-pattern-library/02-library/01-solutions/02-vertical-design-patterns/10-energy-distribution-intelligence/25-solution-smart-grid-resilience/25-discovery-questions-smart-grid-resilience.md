# Discovery Questions: Smart Grid Resilience & Self-Healing

## Discovery & Current State Assessment

1. **What is your current grid outage frequency and duration (SAIDI/SAIFI)?**
   - *Purpose: Establish baseline reliability metrics*

2. **How do you currently detect and respond to grid faults?**
   - *Purpose: Assess current fault management maturity*

3. **What percentage of fault response requires manual intervention?**
   - *Purpose: Identify automation opportunities*
