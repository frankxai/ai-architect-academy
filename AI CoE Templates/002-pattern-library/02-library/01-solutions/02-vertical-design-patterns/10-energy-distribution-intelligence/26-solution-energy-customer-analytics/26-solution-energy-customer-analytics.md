# Pattern 26: Energy Customer Analytics & Engagement

## Solution Overview

The Energy Customer Analytics & Engagement pattern leverages AI-driven insights from smart meter data, behavioral analytics, and personalized engagement to achieve 45% improvement in customer satisfaction, 30% increase in energy efficiency program participation, and 25% reduction in customer churn. This pattern transforms utilities from commodity providers into customer-centric energy partners through hyper-personalized recommendations, proactive engagement, and value-added services.

## Business Problem

Energy utilities face evolving customer expectations and competitive pressures:

- **Customer Dissatisfaction**: Only 65% customer satisfaction with utilities
- **Low Program Participation**: <20% enrollment in efficiency programs
- **High Churn Rates**: 15% annual churn in deregulated markets
- **Generic Communications**: One-size-fits-all customer messaging
- **Limited Insights**: Underutilized smart meter data (using <5%)
- **Rising Expectations**: Digital-native customer demands
- **Competition**: New energy retailers and service providers
- **Revenue Pressure**: Declining consumption per customer
