## Discovery & Current State Assessment

1. **What energy commodities and markets do you currently trade, and what is your average daily trading volume?**
   - *Purpose: Understand trading scope, scale, and market coverage requirements*

2. **What trading platforms, order management systems, and market data feeds do you use today?**
   - *Purpose: Assess current technology stack and integration requirements*

3. **How do you currently execute trades - manually, semi-automated, or fully algorithmic?**
   - *Purpose: Determine automation maturity and transformation scope*

## Strategy & Business Alignment

4. **What are your primary trading strategies (arbitrage, hedging, speculation, market making)?**

6. **How do you measure trading performance today, and what KPIs are most important?**
