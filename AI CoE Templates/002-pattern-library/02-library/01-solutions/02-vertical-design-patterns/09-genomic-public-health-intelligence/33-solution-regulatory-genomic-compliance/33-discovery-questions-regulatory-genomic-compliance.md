# Discovery Questions: Regulatory Genomic Compliance & Reporting

## Discovery & Current State Assessment

1. **What types of genomic data do you currently process, and what are the volumes?**
   - *Purpose: Understand data scope, scale, and complexity for compliance requirements*

2. **Which regulatory frameworks and standards must you comply with (HIPAA, GDPR, CLIA, ISO 15189)?**
   - *Purpose: Map specific regulatory requirements and compliance obligations*

3. **How do you currently manage consent for genomic data collection, use, and sharing?**
   - *Purpose: Assess consent management maturity and gaps*

---

## Strategy & Business Alignment

4. **What are your primary drivers for enhancing genomic compliance (regulatory changes, audit findings, international expansion)?**
   - *Purpose: Understand business drivers and urgency for compliance improvements*

5. **Which stakeholders are involved in compliance decisions (legal, clinical, research, IT)?**
   - *Purpose: Identify decision makers and governance structure*

6. **How would automated compliance impact your genomic operations and research capabilities?**
   - *Purpose: Establish value proposition and success metrics*

---

## Opportunity Qualification

7. **Have you experienced any compliance violations, audit findings, or data breaches?**
   - *Purpose: Understand risk exposure and compliance history*

8. **What is your budget for compliance infrastructure and ongoing operations?**
   - *Purpose: Qualify financial commitment and scope appropriately*

9. **What is your timeline for achieving full regulatory compliance?**
   - *Purpose: Understand urgency and project timeline constraints*

---

## Technical Scoping & Architecture

10. **What laboratory information management systems (LIMS) do you currently use?**
    - *Purpose: Plan integration requirements with existing laboratory systems*

11. **How is genomic data currently stored, and what are your retention requirements?**
    - *Purpose: Design appropriate data storage and lifecycle management*

12. **What bioinformatics pipelines and analysis tools do you use?**
    - *Purpose: Assess integration needs with analytical workflows*

---

## Data Governance & Privacy

13. **How do you currently handle de-identification and anonymization of genomic data?**
    - *Purpose: Evaluate privacy protection mechanisms and improvements needed*

14. **What are your data sovereignty and localization requirements?**
    - *Purpose: Design compliant data residency architecture*

15. **How do you manage access control and authentication for genomic data?**
    - *Purpose: Assess identity management and access control maturity*

---

## Audit & Reporting

16. **What types of regulatory reports do you currently submit, and to which authorities?**
    - *Purpose: Map reporting requirements and automation opportunities*

17. **How do you maintain audit trails for genomic data processing and access?**
    - *Purpose: Evaluate audit trail completeness and integrity*

18. **What is your current process for preparing for regulatory audits?**
    - *Purpose: Identify audit preparation pain points and automation needs*

---

## International Collaboration

19. **Do you share genomic data internationally, and with which organizations?**
    - *Purpose: Understand cross-border data transfer requirements*

20. **Which international genomic data sharing initiatives do you participate in (GISAID, GA4GH)?**
    - *Purpose: Plan for international standard compliance*

21. **How do you manage different consent requirements across jurisdictions?**
    - *Purpose: Design multi-jurisdictional consent management*

---

## Quality Management

22. **What laboratory accreditations do you hold or seek (CLIA, CAP, ISO 15189)?**
    - *Purpose: Align compliance platform with accreditation requirements*

23. **How do you manage proficiency testing and external quality assessments?**
    - *Purpose: Integrate quality management workflows*

24. **What is your document control and SOP management process?**
    - *Purpose: Plan document management integration*

---

## Clinical Integration

25. **How do genomic results integrate with electronic health records?**
    - *Purpose: Design clinical integration and reporting workflows*

26. **What is your process for handling incidental findings and secondary findings?**
    - *Purpose: Implement appropriate ethical and regulatory controls*

27. **How do you manage familial implications of genetic findings?**
    - *Purpose: Design family data management and consent workflows*

---

## Research Compliance

28. **Do you use genomic data for research, and how is consent managed?**
    - *Purpose: Separate clinical and research consent workflows*

29. **How do you manage Institutional Review Board (IRB) approvals?**
    - *Purpose: Integrate IRB workflow management*

30. **What are your data sharing requirements for publications and research?**
    - *Purpose: Implement compliant data sharing mechanisms*

---

## Security & Risk Management

31. **What security measures protect genomic data currently?**
    - *Purpose: Assess security posture and enhancement needs*

32. **How do you handle genomic data breaches or security incidents?**
    - *Purpose: Design incident response procedures*

33. **What are your disaster recovery and business continuity requirements?**
    - *Purpose: Plan high availability and recovery architecture*

---

## Operational Considerations

34. **How many users need access to the compliance platform?**
    - *Purpose: Size user access and training requirements*

35. **What are your requirements for compliance dashboards and reporting?**
    - *Purpose: Design monitoring and reporting interfaces*

36. **Do you need 24/7 compliance monitoring and support?**
    - *Purpose: Plan operational support model*

---

## Future Considerations

37. **Are you planning to expand genomic testing services or geographic coverage?**
    - *Purpose: Design scalable compliance architecture*

38. **How do you plan to handle emerging genomic technologies (long-read sequencing, single-cell)?**
    - *Purpose: Ensure platform flexibility for new technologies*

39. **What role will AI/ML play in your genomic analysis, and what are the compliance implications?**
    - *Purpose: Address AI governance and explainability requirements*

---

*These discovery questions are designed to comprehensively assess an organization's readiness for implementing the Regulatory Genomic Compliance & Reporting pattern. The responses will help determine the appropriate implementation approach, identify regulatory requirements, and customize the solution to meet specific genomic compliance needs.*
