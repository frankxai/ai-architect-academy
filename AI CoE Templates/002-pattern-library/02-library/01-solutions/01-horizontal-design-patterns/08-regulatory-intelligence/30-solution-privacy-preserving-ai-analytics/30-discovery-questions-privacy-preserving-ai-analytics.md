## Strategic and Business Questions

### Business Objectives and Privacy Requirements
1. **Primary Privacy Challenges**
   - What are your most significant data privacy challenges that prevent or limit collaborative analytics and data sharing?
   - Which business opportunities are you currently unable to pursue due to privacy regulations or data sovereignty requirements?
   - How do current privacy regulations (GDPR, CCPA, HIPAA, etc.) impact your data analytics and AI initiatives?
   - What level of privacy protection do you need to enable new business models and partnerships?

2. **Collaboration and Data Sharing Needs**
   - What collaborative analytics opportunities would provide the most business value if privacy concerns could be addressed?
   - Which external organizations (partners, competitors, government agencies) would you like to collaborate with on data analytics?
   - What types of data would you be willing to include in privacy-preserving collaborative analytics?
   - How important is cross-border data collaboration for your business objectives?

3. **Regulatory and Compliance Landscape**
   - Which privacy regulations currently apply to your organization, and how do they impact your data analytics initiatives?
   - What are your biggest compliance concerns regarding data sharing and collaborative analytics?
   - How do you currently demonstrate privacy compliance to regulators and auditors?
   - What would be the business impact of being able to participate in cross-jurisdictional data collaborations?

### Current Data Analytics and AI Maturity

4. **Existing Analytics Capabilities**
   - What is your current data analytics and AI maturity level, and what types of analytics do you currently perform?
   - Which data sources and datasets would be most valuable for privacy-preserving analytics?
   - What machine learning and AI models are you currently using that could benefit from privacy-preserving techniques?
   - How do you currently handle data privacy in your analytics workflows?

5. **Data Governance and Privacy Practices**
   - What data governance frameworks and privacy policies do you currently have in place?
   - How do you currently classify and handle sensitive data in your organization?
   - What consent management and data subject rights processes do you have implemented?
   - How do you currently assess and manage privacy risks in data analytics projects?

## Technical Infrastructure Questions

### Current Technology Stack and Capabilities

6. **Data Infrastructure and Platforms**
   - What data platforms, databases, and analytics tools do you currently use?
   - How is your data currently stored, processed, and secured across different systems?
   - What cloud platforms and services do you use, and what are your data residency requirements?
   - Do you have experience with advanced cryptography, secure computation, or privacy-preserving technologies?

7. **Security and Cryptographic Capabilities**
   - What cryptographic tools and technologies do you currently use for data protection?
   - Do you have hardware security modules (HSMs) or trusted execution environments (TEEs) in your infrastructure?
   - What is your current approach to key management and cryptographic operations?
   - How do you currently implement end-to-end encryption and secure communications?

8. **AI/ML Infrastructure and Expertise**
   - What machine learning platforms and frameworks do you currently use?
   - Do you have experience with federated learning, distributed ML, or collaborative AI techniques?
   - What is your team's expertise level with advanced AI/ML techniques and cryptographic privacy methods?
   - How do you currently manage ML model training, deployment, and monitoring?

### Integration and Interoperability Requirements

9. **System Integration Needs**
   - How would privacy-preserving analytics need to integrate with your existing data and analytics infrastructure?
   - What APIs, data formats, and integration protocols would be required for seamless integration?
   - Do you need to integrate with external partners' systems, and what are their technical requirements?
   - What are your requirements for real-time versus batch privacy-preserving analytics?

10. **Performance and Scalability Requirements**
    - What are your performance requirements for privacy-preserving analytics in terms of computation time and throughput?
    - How much computational overhead is acceptable for privacy-preserving techniques compared to traditional analytics?
    - What data volumes and user concurrency levels do you need to support?
    - Do you have specific latency requirements for collaborative analytics or multi-party computation?

## Privacy and Cryptographic Requirements

### Privacy-Preserving Techniques and Preferences

11. **Federated Learning Requirements**
    - What types of machine learning models would benefit from federated learning across multiple organizations?
    - How many parties would typically participate in federated learning collaborations?
    - What are your requirements for model accuracy and performance in federated settings?
    - How should intellectual property and model ownership be handled in federated learning scenarios?

12. **Differential Privacy Needs**
    - What types of statistical queries and analytics would benefit from differential privacy guarantees?
    - How do you want to balance privacy protection with analytical utility and accuracy?
    - What level of privacy budget management and tracking capability do you need?
    - How should privacy parameters be set and managed across different types of analyses?

13. **Homomorphic Encryption Applications**
    - What types of computations on encrypted data would be most valuable for your use cases?
    - How important is the ability to perform complex analytics on encrypted data without decryption?
    - What are your performance requirements for homomorphic encryption operations?
    - Which data types and computational operations are most critical for encrypted computation?

14. **Secure Multi-Party Computation Use Cases**
    - What types of collaborative computations would you want to perform without sharing raw data?
    - How many parties would typically be involved in secure multi-party computation scenarios?
    - What types of results and insights should be revealed to different parties?
    - How should trust and verification be established among computation participants?

### Trust and Verification Requirements

15. **Trust Models and Assumptions**
    - What level of trust do you have in potential collaboration partners for privacy-preserving analytics?
    - How important are cryptographic guarantees versus trust-based privacy protection?
    - What verification and audit capabilities do you need for privacy-preserving computations?
    - How should disputes or disagreements be resolved in multi-party privacy-preserving analytics?

16. **Transparency and Explainability**
    - How important is the ability to explain and audit privacy-preserving analytics results?
    - What level of transparency do you need about the privacy techniques and parameters used?
    - How should zero-knowledge proofs and cryptographic verification be integrated into workflows?
    - What documentation and evidence do you need for regulatory compliance and auditing?

## Compliance and Governance Questions

### Regulatory Compliance Requirements

17. **Privacy Regulation Compliance**
    - Which specific privacy regulations must be addressed by privacy-preserving analytics (GDPR, CCPA, HIPAA, etc.)?
    - What are your requirements for demonstrating compliance with privacy regulations through technical measures?
    - How do you need to handle data subject rights (access, deletion, portability) in privacy-preserving systems?
    - What consent management and purpose limitation requirements apply to collaborative analytics?

18. **Data Sovereignty and Residency**
    - What data sovereignty and residency requirements must be maintained in privacy-preserving analytics?
    - Are there restrictions on where data can be processed or computations can be performed?
    - How should cross-border data transfers be handled while maintaining sovereignty requirements?
    - What jurisdictional controls and audit capabilities are required for international collaborations?

19. **Industry-Specific Regulations**
    - What industry-specific regulations and standards apply to your privacy-preserving analytics initiatives?
    - Are there specific requirements for financial services, healthcare, government, or other regulated industries?
    - How do you need to demonstrate compliance with industry standards and best practices?
    - What certification or accreditation requirements apply to privacy-preserving technologies?

### Governance and Risk Management

20. **Privacy Risk Assessment and Management**
    - How do you currently assess and manage privacy risks in data analytics projects?
    - What privacy impact assessment processes would be needed for privacy-preserving analytics?
    - How should privacy risks be monitored and managed in ongoing collaborative analytics?
    - What escalation and incident response procedures are needed for privacy-related issues?

21. **Data Ethics and Responsible AI**
    - What ethical frameworks and principles guide your data analytics and AI initiatives?
    - How should fairness, bias, and discrimination be addressed in privacy-preserving analytics?
    - What transparency and accountability requirements apply to AI models trained with privacy-preserving techniques?
    - How should algorithmic decision-making be explained and justified when using privacy-preserving methods?
