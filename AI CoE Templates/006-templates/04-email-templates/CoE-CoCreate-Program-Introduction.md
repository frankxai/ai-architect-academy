### What is the Co-Create Program?
The Co-Create Program is a structured engagement model where our AI CoE partners directly with business units to jointly develop, prototype, and deploy AI-powered solutions. Rather than a traditional vendor-client relationship, we work as integrated partners throughout the entire journey from ideation to production.

### Key Program Benefits
• **Rapid Prototyping** - Transform concepts into working prototypes in weeks, not months  
• **Expert Guidance** - Direct access to AI architects, data scientists, and ML engineers  
• **Risk Mitigation** - Shared investment model reduces individual project risk  
• **Knowledge Transfer** - Build internal AI capabilities through hands-on collaboration  
• **Proven Methodology** - Leverage our established frameworks and best practices  

### How It Works
1. **Discovery Phase** (Week 1-2): Joint exploration of use cases and value opportunities
2. **Design Sprint** (Week 3-4): Collaborative solution design and feasibility assessment  
3. **Prototype Development** (Week 5-8): Agile development with continuous stakeholder feedback
4. **Pilot Deployment** (Week 9-12): Controlled rollout with performance monitoring
5. **Scale & Optimize**: Production deployment with ongoing support and enhancement
