Chunking

Segment content at semantic boundaries to balance recall and precision.

Quick Wins

  • Target 200–400 tokens per chunk; adjust based on corpus density.
  • Use heading-aware or semantic chunkers for structured documents.
  • Overlap chunks slightly to avoid cutting references mid-sentence.
  • Log retrieval hit rate and iterate with evaluations.
